Developing Gas Detecting Alarm Safety and AutoExhaust Control System With Automatic Shut-off Valve.

Aug 2023 – Oct 2023

Project Description

Designed and implemented an intelligent gas leakage detection and safety system using MQ-series gas sensors and an ESP32 microcontroller. The system automatically detects hazardous gas levels, activates alarms, and controls exhaust fans for ventilation. An integrated automatic shut-off valve cuts off gas supply during critical leaks to prevent accidents. Real-time alerts were sent over MQTT to a central monitoring dashboard. The setup was optimized for household and industrial safety automation.
